Analytics Hub has been deployed!

To access JupyterHub:

  kubectl port-forward -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} svc/proxy-public 8000:80

Then open http://localhost:8000

To check hub pod status:

  kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l component=hub

To view hub logs:

  kubectl logs -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l component=hub
